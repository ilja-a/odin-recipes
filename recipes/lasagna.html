Lasagna Recipe

Here is a lasagna recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent.
  3. Add the ground beef and Italian sausage to the skillet. Cook until browned, breaking it up into small pieces with a wooden spoon.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and half of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top.
  8. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by another layer of meat sauce. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  9. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the lasagna.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  12. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve hot.
