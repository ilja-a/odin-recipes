Tomato Soup Recipe

Here is a tomato soup recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans (14 ounces each) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, butter, and dried basil.
  2. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Once simmering, reduce the heat to low and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Continue cooking for an additional 5 minutes, then remove from heat.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
